INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to beloved Bikini Bottom!

Once you click the PLAY GAME button, you will be given your very own Tomagotchi. Your job is to keep your Tomagotchi happy, healthy, awake, and most of all ALIVE!

A display will appear in the upper left corner of the screen that will show you the different levels of the Tomagotchi's stats. All of them will begin at zero and increase at different intervals. Click the buttons that correspond to each stat in order to keep healthy levels of each. If any of the stats reach 10, or you go below zero, YOU LOSE!

Now that you have the basics down, go enjoy your time in Bikini Bottom!
